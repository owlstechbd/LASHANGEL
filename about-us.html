About Us

Welcome to Lash Angel

MASTER LASH TECHNICIANS

Lash Angel are dedicated lash experts trained with a few of the UK industry most reputable leaders in eyelash extensions. We are certified by Nouveau Lashes, Lash Perfect and the Beauty Academy. With our combined skills and expertise we are able to provide you the sensational semi-permanent eyelash extensions, both in Classic and Russian Volume techniques. This relaxing treatment has been around for decades and is now here to stay. We have over 12 years in the lash industry and can tailor the lashes to your exact requirments. Due to our high level of expertise we can apply these to your lashes in a way that are completely damage-free. You are now able to achieve the long, beautiful eyelashes you have always dreamed of.

Semi-permanent eyelashes effortlessly emphasise the shape of the eye, creating an attractive, stunning and captivating effect. Short, sparse or thin lashes are instantly transformed into long voluminous gorgeous lashes.

We also offer LVL Lashes which straightens, lifts and lengthens your natural lashes, this procedure gives you " the wide awake look which is completely 100% natural and is really yours", suitable for those who have downward lashes - they can lift the lashes very effectively to compliment lash extensions, to straighten out curly lashes or for lucky individuals who don't need eyelash extensions and just needs a little more lift without the need for eyelash curlers. The benefit of LVL lashes is that they can be used with or without lash extensions.
